I've been using ChatGPT for a short time to help me with various things. I feel like I was a little late to get on board with AI, but it's been really helpful for things like organizing my thoughts and creating a clear path (aka clear rules, which my brain loves) to get things done.

It becaomes pretty clear pretty quickly that AI has a certain cadence to its responses. 

It's not just a cadence. It's a way of speaking so people listen. 

See what I did there. 

I can't help now but read social media content in the same voice and cadence I read chat responses and I can't tell if a large chunk of social media is people using AI to write a the post of if it's in their own voice. 

And I'm not just talking about the influencery types. 

I'm talking regular folks just posting about their life. A trip they took. A thing they did. A person they met. 

If I read your post and I can read it in my AI voice and it fits - I'm leaning towards it being AI. 

And no offense! I'll read my own stuff and can sometimes hear the AI vibe in my words. 

No clue what to do about it, although I am really starting to be selective with what comes in my feed, especilally when it's the influencery-types that feel too AI too consistently. 

When I follow a person or a page, I want it to be real. And raw. I want thier own words! Not some polished AI output. 

Also, unpopular opinion, I have no problem with AI. 

It's a thing. 

It's happening. 

Probably good not to be a ludite.
